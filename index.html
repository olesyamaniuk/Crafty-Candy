Game Description

Welcome to Crafty Candy - Match 3 Game, a spellbinding puzzle adventure where candy magic meets match-3 excitement! Join Candice, the friendly apprentice witch, and her cute kitten, Cookie, as they explore an enchanted world filled with sweet surprises and magical puzzles.
Match three or more candies to unleash powerful spells and clear obstacles on your way to victory! From marshmallow madness to gooey chocolate waterfalls, every level is a new, tasty challenge. Whether you're looking to relax or test your puzzle-solving skills, Crafty Candy offers an enchanting experience for players of all ages!
